a bit about BrewsCruise...

I'm a fan of hot springs. And craft beer. And combining both for an even better time. And I'm betting you might be a fan too.

BrewsCruise is meant to be a community resource for folks like you and I. Simply a list of hot springs in Colorado, for now, maybe more states in the future, anyway, a list of hot springs and important details like latitude, longitude, address, website, and nearby breweries.

If you don't see your favorite soaking spot on the list feel free to add it with the form on the home page! Also, if there's a hot spring with missing info and you're in the know, update its listing and share that knowledge with the rest of us!
